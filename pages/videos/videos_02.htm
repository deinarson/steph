L'usine America Mon Amour
Falling Fashion
Empty?  
 
  America Mon Amour, 2005-2009, 8 minutes.
Video animation. Made from 50 drawings of 5 x 8 cm each. A black & white animation made of drawings related to the history of the American continent and is inspired by the French movie Hiroshima Mon Amour. The characters; a North American autochtone and a South American of mix descend, are revealed to us through a narration. Sharing stories about their torture and torment and the abuse of authorities we see the effects of colonization.
