Statement

I use paper and video in order to create a body of works and installations to comment on society. With drawing, collage, photo, animation, video and installation, I tell stories in series searching to transcend mediums and dimensions

My work evolves towards minimalism with cut-outs silhouettes and their shadows that creates a dramatic aspect. I introduce movement in my 2D artworks using presentations in sequences, inspired by cinema and graphic novels. With a dark humor, I comment History, environment and current events mixing 2D & 3D; drawing, photo and video while mixing as well a naïve aesthetic with troubling subjects.


Biography

Stéphanie Morissette holds a BFA in Art History and Studio Art, and a DESS in International Artistic Cooperation. During the past 15 years, she has been involved in several art organizations and festivals and worked on her art projects during artist residencies at the Banff Centre in Alberta, the Straumur Art Commune in Iceland and the Tapiola Guest Studio in Finland. Stéphanie has produced several projects utilising drawing, video and installation. Her work has been exhibited internationally since 2002; her drawing animation Fashion has been selected and presented for the program Nightcomers at the 10th International Istanbul Biennial of Contemporary Art in Turkey (2007); for her work Executive Power, she received an honorary mention at the 2013 Just For Laughs International Visual Arts Contest in Montréal. She lives at the Lezarts artists cooperative, her last video animation América mon amour (América My Love) is distributed by Vidéographe and her work is represented by the gallery Dominique Bouffard in Montréal.
